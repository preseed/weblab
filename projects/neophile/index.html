NEOPHILE





Someone who loves everything new or novel.
Showing distaste or downright loathing
of tradition, repetition, and routine.

What would you create if you find a network of brains like yours? Would you do your stupid
job or rather change the world? Pre-
modern societies tend to be suspicious
of the novel. It is a feature of modernity
that we are addicted to novelty.
